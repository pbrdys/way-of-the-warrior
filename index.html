Wudang Kung Fu

Way of the Warrior

... is a center for internal martial, healing, and movement arts, strongly inspired by the ancestral principles of the Wudang School in China. Our training contains internal martial arts such as Taiji Quan, Bagua Zhang, Xing Yi Quan as well as the famous Wudang Swordsmanship, and Longpole techniques. Qi Gong (inner work) along with various Kung Fu foundational exercises form the cornerstone of our practice.
